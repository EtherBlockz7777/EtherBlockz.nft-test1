SHORT FACTS

Community Based

The project is 100% community based. The community decides what happens to the Etherpool over the course of the project. This is evaluated via votes on our socials. For example Sweeping Floor, After Mint Marketing or Holders Meeting and much more. We will builing the strongest community throughout the scene!
 

3D & AR

All Etherblockz are modeled and constructed in 3D. All holders have exclusiv access to our Block-Viewer. Etherblockz can be viewed in 3D and AR in the Block-Viewer. A preview of the Block-Viewer can be found in the 3D & AR section.

Artwork

An NFT collective designed by austrian artists consisting of 7,777 unique 1-in-1 3D collectibles living on the Ethereum Blockchain as ERC-721 tokens. All Etherblockz are rendered form an 3D gltf file to a PNG image. Each image has a resolution of 1500px X 1500px so it can be displayed in detail on any device Aesthetics, community and quality are our top priorities.

ROADMAP

1. Preparations

Blockz are born. Our two founders and artists developed and designed Blockz's branding, art style, metadata and generative NFT attributes.
Our community is becoming an invasion of unstoppable blocks ready to capitalize.

2. Start Marketing

A full-scale offensive to expand our invasion begins.
Our marketing director uses all sorts of strategies to prepare people for the Etherblockz invasion.
Blockz uses high profile influencers and other effective marketing strategies to break into social platforms like Twitter and Instagram.
NFT Airdrops are sent to key allies.

3. Chill Out Area | More Sneak-Peaks

We will set up our discord server where Blockz can communicate with each other and chill out.
Our art director is preparing some sneak peaks for our block community.
Including multiple 3D and AR insights.

4. First Community Challenges

Our community manager gives the starting signal for the first AR challenge.
Winners of this multi-day challenge will receive whitelist spots and more.
The founders are announcing several important project details these days.

5. Mint Phase

The community is getting bigger and bigger.
Now all the details about the mint will be revealed to the community.
Our blockchain developer prepares the smart contract for the erc-721 tokens, uploads all blocks to the ethereum network and builds the minting page.
Now the white list members have the possibility to secure an ether block.
The public sale starts 24 hours after the whitelist sale if there are still enough blocks left.
WE ARE READY!
The first details about the Baby-Blockz will be revealed.

6. After Mint

The mint is over. Now let's focus on after mint marketing.
The community now decides what will happen with the ether pool via polls.
We will support the community to sweep the floor price.
Further information will be announced via our socials.
BLOCKZ TO THE MOON!
